🔍 Threshold Finding

Error rate never exceeded 0% — even at concurrency=150, the system did not drop requests. However, P95 latency for DB-heavy endpoints (/conversations, /agents) crossed 1.8 seconds at concurrency ≥110, making them impractical for real-time UIs.

  • /api/config (lightweight, no DB): Stable ~880-1000 QPS, P95 stays under 0.3s even at 150 concurrency

  • /agents (DB read): P95 jumps from 0.07s (C=10) → 1.88s (C=150) — similar degradation
  • Practical concurrency threshold: ~70-90 (P95 latency under 1.2s for DB endpoints)
